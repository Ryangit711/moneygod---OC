## STRATEGY 1: OPENING RANGE BREAKOUT (ORB) — USD/CAD

**Best For:** NY session (5am-10am PT)
**Timeframe:** 15-min entry, 1H structure
**Pairs:** USD/CAD (primary), EUR/USD (secondary)

### The Setup

```
1. 9:30 AM ET = NY open → let first 15-min candle complete (9:30-9:45)
2. Mark the HIGH and LOW of that candle → this is your ORB range
3. Wait for price to break above HIGH (long) or below LOW (short)
4. Confirm with volume (above average)
5. Enter 1 tick beyond the breakout level
6. Stop loss: opposite side of the ORB range
7. Target: 20 pips (1:2 risk/reward)
```

### Entry Rules

**Long:**
- Price breaks above ORB high AND closes above on 1-min chart
- Volume above average
- Overall bias not strongly bearish

**Short:**
- Price breaks below ORB low AND closes below on 1-min chart
- Volume above average
- Overall bias not strongly bullish

### Risk
- Stop = opposite side of ORB (or max 10 pips)
- Risk ≤ 1% of account
- Never move stop further away

### Targets
- TP1: +10 pips (optional partial close at 50%)
- TP2: +20 pips (main target)
- If strong trend, trail behind swing lows/highs

### Rules
- Max 3 trades/day across all pairs
- No revenge trades after 2 consecutive losses — stand down
- Skip if first 15-min bar is absurdly large (spike = chop)

## STRATEGY 2: LONDON BREAKOUT — EUR/USD

**Best For:** London session (midnight-3am PT) or London-NY overlap (5am-8am PT)

### The Setup

```
1. Mark the Asian session high and low (12am-4am London time)
2. Wait for London open (midnight PT)
3. When price breaks above Asian high → long
   When price breaks below Asian low → short
4. Confirm with volume increase (bigger candles, faster ticks)
5. Entry: 5-10 pips beyond the Asian range boundary
6. Stop loss: opposite side of Asian range
7. Target: 30-50 pips
```

### Entry Rules — Breakout

**Long:**
- Price breaks ABOVE Asian high by 5-10 pips and closes above
- Volume/volatility increases (larger candles, faster ticks)

**Short:**
- Price breaks BELOW Asian low by 5-10 pips and closes below
- Same volatility confirmation

### Entry Rules — Reversal (Optional)

- At prior day high/low, look for strong rejection wick + close back inside level
- Enter toward mean (back into range) with tight stop

### Risk
- Default stop: 15-25 pips
- Risk ≤ 1% of account
- No adding to losers

### Targets
- TP1: +15-20 pips (1R)
- TP2: +30-50 pips (2R+), trail behind structure

### Rules
- Max 2-3 trades/day across all FX
- Avoid low-liquidity rollover and 5 min around red-flag news
- If EUR/USD is choppy (fake breaks both sides), stop trading it for the day
