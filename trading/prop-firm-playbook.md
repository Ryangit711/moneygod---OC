## DAILY LOSS RULES — The Most Important Number

Know YOUR firm's exact rule. Set your personal max at 50% of their limit.

| Firm | Their Daily Limit | Your Personal Max | Calculation Method |
|------|------------------|------------------|-------------------|
| FTMO | 5% | 2.5% | Equity at 5PM ET prev day minus current equity |
| Funding Pips | 4% | 2% | Balance-based |
| The5ers | 4-5% | 2% | Equity-based |
| MyFundedFutures | None (EOD only) | 2% self-imposed | N/A — only max drawdown matters |
| Apex | Trailing | 2% self-imposed | Highest equity minus current equity |
| TradeDay | EOD | 2% self-imposed | End-of-day balance |
| Tradeify | EOD | 2% self-imposed | End-of-day balance |

**Rule:** The firm's limit is the ABSOLUTE MAX. Your personal limit is 50% of that. You never want to hit their limit — only your own.
