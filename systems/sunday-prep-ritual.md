# SUNDAY PREP RITUAL — Weekly Chart Context

## "The daily chart tells you what happened. The weekly chart tells you what's coming."

---

## FRAMING (Why This Exists)

The syllabus teaches intraday patterns (5-min, 15-min). But every intraday move happens inside a higher timeframe structure that is set before the week begins. Without the weekly context, you're trading patterns in a vacuum — you see the sweep but don't know if it's a trend continuation or a range boundary.

**Time:** 20 minutes. Sunday evening. Before the first trading day of the week.

---

## THE 20-MINUTE RITUAL

### MIN 1-5: OPEN THE WEEKLY CHART

| Instrument | What to Check | Mark These |
|-----------|--------------|------------|
| **MES / ES (weekly)** | Previous week's high, low, close | Clear horizontal lines at PDH, PDL, PDC |
| **MES / ES (monthly)** | Current month's high, low, open | Wider context — are we near monthly extremes? |
| **DXY (weekly)** | Dollar trend direction | Is DXY up, down, or ranging? This sets your primary bias. |
| **VIX (weekly)** | Volatility regime | < 15, 15-20, 20-25, > 25 — this sets your trade mode for the week |

### MIN 6-10: FIND UNFILLED FVGs (4H + Daily)

Scan the 4H and Daily charts for any unfilled Fair Value Gaps. These are the highest-probability targets for the upcoming week.

| FVG | Timeframe | Location | Direction | Distance from Current Price |
|-----|-----------|----------|-----------|---------------------------|
| #1 | 4H / Daily | | Bullish / Bearish | pts away |
| #2 | 4H / Daily | | Bullish / Bearish | pts away |
| #3 | 4H / Daily | | Bullish / Bearish | pts away |

**Rule:** The closest unfilled FVG is the most likely target for the week. If price is approaching one, write down the entry plan now.

---

### MIN 11-15: IDENTIFY WEEKLY LIQUIDITY POOLS

Mark these on your weekly chart:

| Liquidity Pool | How to Find | What It Means |
|---------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Sellside below prior week low** | PDL on weekly | Retail stops below last week. Institutions may sweep it. |
| **Buyside above prior week high** | PDH on weekly | Retail stops above last week. Institutions may sweep it. |
| **Sellside below prior month low** | Monthly pivot low | Larger pool. Sweeping this has bigger momentum. |
| **Buyside above prior month high** | Monthly pivot high | Larger pool. Sweeping this has bigger momentum. |
| **Round numbers** | 100-point levels on ES (5000, 5100, 5200) | Always have resting stops. Predictive. |

**Which pool is MOST likely to be targeted this week?** (circle one)

> SELLSIDE sweep of _____ / BUYSIDE sweep of _____ / NEITHER (range expected)
