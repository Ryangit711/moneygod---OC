# Global [Monetary Plumbing](13-weekly-flow-tapping-operating-system.md): How [Money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) Enters the System (Country-Agnostic)

> **Core Principle**: [Central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) creates [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) → [commercial [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](03-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)s issue [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) → that [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) becomes the [money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) stock. No nation names. Only functions.

## II. Step-by-Step: From Reserve Creation to Deposit Circulation

1. **[Central [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) Operation**
   - Asset purchase (government bonds, mortgage-backed securities, corporate paper, FX) **OR** lending facility (discount window, repo, standing facility).
   - Pays by [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)ing the selling [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)'s **reserve account** at the [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md).
   - [Central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) balance sheet: **Assets ↑ | [Reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) (liability) ↑**.

2. **[Commercial [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](03-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) Response**
   - Holds new [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md). Can now meet regulatory [liquidity](13-weekly-flow-tapping-operating-system.md) ratios (LCR, reserve requirements) more easily.
   - Extends a new loan: creates a **loan asset** and a **deposit liability** simultaneously (double-entry).
   - No prior reserve needed for the *individual* loan; system-wide reserve adequacy matters.

3. **Deposit Enters Economy**
   - Borrower spends the deposit → recipient's [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) receives [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) via inter[bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) settlement.
   - [Reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) **shuffle** between [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)s; total system [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) unchanged (unless [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) drains/injects).
   - Deposit velocity × stock = nominal spending (NGDP proxy).

4. **Leakages & Drains**
   - **Currency withdrawal**: public converts deposits to cash → [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) ↓, cash in circulation ↑.
   - **[Central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) reverse repo / asset sales / rate hikes**: [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) ↓.
   - **Cross-border outflows**: [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) leave the domestic system (FX intervention sterilization).
   - **Loan repayment / write-offs**: deposits destroyed, broad [money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) ↓.

5. **Feedback to [Central [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md)**
   - [Central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) watches: [inflation](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md), [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) growth, asset prices, exchange rate.
   - Adjusts policy rate / balance sheet → influences cost & availability of [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) → feeds back to step 1.

## III. Country-Agnostic Taxonomy of Major Economies

For each economy below, the **structure is identical**; only scale, institutional labels, and policy tools differ.

| Economy Tier | [Central [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) Function | [Commercial [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](03-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) System | Distinctive Plumbing Feature |
|--------------|----------------------|------------------------|------------------------------|
| **Reserve-Currency Issuer (Tier 1)** | Issues the **global reserve asset**; its [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) are held worldwide. Operates swap lines to other [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md)s. | Deep, globally connected; large USD/EUR/GBP/JPY/CHF funding markets. | **Exorbitant privilege**: can export its currency; foreign demand for its [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) dampens domestic [inflation](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) from [money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) growth. |
| **Major Non-Reserve Advanced (Tier 2)** | Issues a **freely floated, internationally used** currency. Holds own FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md). | Well-capitalized; significant domestic bond markets. | **Swap-line access** to Tier 1; currency acts as regional anchor. |
| **Emerging Market with Managed Float (Tier 3)** | Actively manages FX rate; accumulates large FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md). Sterilizes inflows via domestic bond sales / reserve requirements. | Often state-influenced; dollarized loan books common. | **Dual anchor**: domestic [inflation](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) target + exchange-rate stability. Sterilization creates domestic interest-rate distortion. |
| **Commodity-Exporter (Tier 4)** | Revenue heavily tied to commodity price (oil, gas, metals, ag). Builds sovereign wealth funds. | [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)ing sector linked to commodity cycles; [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) booms/busts track export revenue. | **Terms-of-trade channel**: export windfall → FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) ↑ → domestic [liquidity](13-weekly-flow-tapping-operating-system.md) ↑ unless sterilized. |
| **Currency-Board / Hard Peg (Tier 5)** | No independent monetary policy; issues domestic currency **only** against foreign reserve asset (1:1 or fixed ratio). | [Credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) growth strictly limited by reserve backing. | **Automatic adjustment**: [money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) supply = FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) × fixed multiplier. No lender-of-last-resort in domestic currency. |
| **Dollarized / Euroized (Tier 6)** | No domestic [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) [money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) creation; uses foreign currency as legal tender. | [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)s are effectively branches of foreign system; [liquidity](13-weekly-flow-tapping-operating-system.md) depends on foreign [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md). | **Imported monetary policy**; domestic [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) = function of foreign [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) + cross-border flows. |

> **Universal Rule**: Every tier follows *Layer 1 → Layer 2* mechanics. Differences are **constraints** (FX regime, reserve currency status, commodity dependence), not **mechanics**.

## IV. Global Flow Map: How a Reserve Injection in One Tier Ripples Outward

```
[Tier 1 [Central [Bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) expands [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md)]
        │
        ├─→ Domestic [commercial [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](03-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)s lend more → domestic deposits ↑ → domestic demand ↑
        │
        ├─→ Swap lines / FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) flow to Tier 2 & 3 [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md)s
        │       └─→ Their [commercial [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](03-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)s get cheaper FX funding → [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) ↑ in those economies
        │
        ├─→ Portfolio rebalancing: global investors sell Tier 1 bonds → buy Tier 2/3/4 assets
        │       └─→ Asset prices ↑, yields ↓ abroad → easier financial conditions globally
        │
        ├─→ Commodity pricing (oil, gold, food) denominated in Tier 1 currency → price ↑
        │       └─→ Tier 4 export revenue ↑ → their FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) ↑ → domestic [liquidity](13-weekly-flow-tapping-operating-system.md) ↑
        │
        └─→ Exchange-rate pressure on Tier 3/5/6
                5/6
                └─→ They intervene (sell FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md), raise rates, tighten macroprudential)
                        └─→ Their domestic [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) ↓ or rates ↑ → [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) growth slows
```

**Feedback Loop**: Slower [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) in Tier 3-6 → weaker import demand → Tier 1 export growth slows → Tier 1 [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) may pause/reverse.

## V. [Oikonomia](02-the-real-meaning-of-economy.md) Lens: [Household Management](02-the-real-meaning-of-economy.md) at Planetary Scale

| [Oikonomia](02-the-real-meaning-of-economy.md) Question | Plumbing Answer |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| **[Who manages the household](12-who-knows-this-level.md)?** | [Central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md)s (Tier 1 collectively, Tier 2-6 nationally) + [commercial [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](03-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) committees. |
| **For whose benefit?** | Tier 1: global asset holders (disproportionately). Tier 2-4: domestic elites + export sectors. Tier 5-6: peg credibility > domestic welfare. |
| **What is the "store of value"?** | Tier 1 [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md) / sovereign [debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md) (global), Tier 2-4 local currency deposits + FX, Tier 5-6 foreign currency cash. |
| **What is the "medium of exchange"?** | [Commercial [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](03-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) deposits (everywhere), cash (declining), stablecoins (emerging). |
| **Where does the surplus go?** | Tier 1: financial assets (equities, real estate, private equity). Tier 2-4: domestic property, sovereign wealth funds, FX [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md). Tier 5-6: foreign [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) deposits. |

> **Insight**: The *[oikonomia](02-the-real-meaning-of-economy.md)* ([household management](02-the-real-meaning-of-economy.md)) is **fragmented** — no single manager. Each tier manages its own sub-household; spillovers are externalities, not coordinated policy.

## X. Actionable Takeaway for the Trader

1. **Watch Tier 1 [central [bank](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md)](05-how-[money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md)-flows-in-the-system.md) balance sheets weekly** (Fed, ECB, BoJ, BoE, SNB, PBOC). Net change = global reserve impulse.
2. **Track swap-line usage & FX swap basis** — signals Tier 2/3 dollar funding stress.
3. **Monitor Tier 4 commodity revenue → FX reserve accrual → domestic [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) impulse** (e.g., Canada oil, Australia iron ore, Brazil soy).
4. **Read Tier 3 sterilization signals** (reverse repo volumes, reserve requirement changes) — they reveal *intent* to slow [credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md).
5. **Position in the pipe**: long assets where [reserves](14-monetary-plumbing-global.md)/[credit](03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md) expand; short where they contract. Use [futures](09-futures-trading-energy-and-receipts.md)/[forex](08-forex-trading-the-purest-flow.md) for direct expression.
