# MONEY CREATION MECHANISM — The Core Engine
## How Each Region Creates Money From Nothing

## THE UNIVERSAL IDENTITY (Country-Agnostic)

```
Δ Broad Money ≈ Δ Commercial Bank Credit

Broad money = cash + bank deposits
Bank credit = loans + mortgages + credit lines
```

Banks do NOT lend deposits. Banks create deposits when they lend.
The deposit comes first. The loan creates it.

For the full mechanics see [14-monetary-plumbing-global.md] and [03-money-as-debt-modern-system.md].

## TRI-REGION MONEY CREATION

### 🇺🇸 US — The Primary Engine

| Layer | Mechanism | Scale | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Fed** | Buys Treasuries/MBS → credits reserves | Up to $9T balance sheet | QE creates reserves, QT destroys them |
| **Banks** | Issue loans → create deposits | M2 ~$21T | 95% of broad money is bank-created |
| **Treasury** | Spending → deposits enter economy | ~$6T/yr budget | Fiscal transfers inject money directly |
| **Money Market** | Repo, CP, Eurodollar | $4T+ daily | Shadow banking creates near-money |

**US Total Broad Money:** ~$21T (M2)
**Annual Growth:** 5-10% (varies with cycle)

**Key Insight:** The US is the only country whose money creation directly drives global liquidity.
When the Fed creates $1, that $1 circulates worldwide — not just in the US.

### 🇨🇦 Canada — The Satellite

| Layer | Mechanism | Scale | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **BoC** | Buys GoC bonds → credits reserves | ~$500B balance sheet | Smaller, follows Fed with lag |
| **Banks** | Issue mortgages → create deposits | M2+ ~$2.5T | **Mortgages are 70% of Canadian bank lending** |
| **Housing** | Home equity extraction → spending | Largest domestic liquidity generator | When house prices rise, homeowners borrow more |

**Canada Total Broad Money:** ~$2.5T (M2+)
**Annual Growth:** 6-12% (housing-driven)

**Key Insight:** Canadian money creation is dominated by housing.
New mortgage → new deposit → new spending → economy runs.
187% household debt-to-income ratio (highest in G7).

### 🇪🇺 Europe — The Fragmented Engine

| Layer | Mechanism | Scale | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **ECB** | Buys bonds (APP, PEPP) → credits reserves | ~€7T balance sheet | Largest CB balance sheet globally |
| **Banks** | Issue loans → create deposits | M3 ~€15T | Slower credit growth than US |
| **TLTRO** | ECB lends cheaply to banks → banks lend | ~€2T outstanding | Targeted lending to real economy |
| **National CBs** | Each country's central bank within Eurosystem | Varies by country | Target2 imbalances show stress |

**Europe Total Broad Money:** ~€15T (M3)
**Annual Growth:** 3-7% (slower than US/Canada)

**Key Insight:** The Eurodollar market is the hidden layer.
European banks also create dollar-denominated credit offshore.
This is the "shadow plumbing" most traders never see.

## PRACTICAL SIGNIFICANCE FOR TRADING

| If This Happens... | ...It Means Money Creation Is... | Trade |
|-------------------|--------------------------------|-------|
| Fed BS expanding, bank lending up, M2 up | **ACCELERATING** globally | Long risk, short USD |
| Fed BS flat, bank lending slowing | **STABLE** | Pick directional trades, range-bound |
| Fed BS shrinking, bank lending contracting | **CONTRACTING** globally | Short risk, long USD, long vol |
| BoC + housing credit expanding | **ACCELERATING locally (Canada)** | Long CAD, long Canadian banks |
| ECB TLTRO repayments + slow lending | **CONTRACTING locally (Europe)** | Short EUR, short European banks |
