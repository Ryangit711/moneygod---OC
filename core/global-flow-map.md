## REGION 1: 🇺🇸 US — THE ENGINE
### Dollar is 88% of FX turnover. Everything else is noise.

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Central Bank** | Federal Reserve — most powerful institution on Earth |
| **Money Creation** | Fed creates reserves → banks create broad money via lending |
| **Liquidity Levers** | Fed Funds Rate, QE/QT, TGA balance, RRP facility, Discount Window, FX swap lines |
| **Currency** | USD — global reserve (59% of global FX reserves), 88% of all FX turnover |
| **What Standardizes** | SOFR, Fed Funds, US Treasury yield curve (the world's risk-free curve) |
| **Key Data** | NFP, CPI, PCE, FOMC, TGA, RRP, Fed balance sheet, JOLTS, retail sales, ISM |
| **Markets** | All USD pairs, SPX/NDX/DJ, US Treasuries (ZN, ZB, ZF, 10Y), WTI, Gold |

**Triggers:**
- Fed cuts → USD ↓ → everything rallies globally
- Fed hikes → USD ↑ → everything sells off globally
- QT accelerates / TGA rising → reserves draining → risk-off
- TGA drawn down / RRP falling → reserves injected → risk-on

**Transmission to LATAM:** When USD weakens → commodity prices ↑ (denominated in USD) → LATAM commodity exporters (BR, MX, CL, CO) rally. EM carry trade active. When USD strengthens → LATAM currencies sell off hardest (highest beta to USD).

**Transmission to Africa:** USD strength → ZAR, NGN sell off. Commodity prices drop → SA mining sector suffers. USD weakness → commodity boom → ZAR rallies.

**Transmission to SE Asia:** USD weak → IDR, MYR, THB, VND all strengthen. Trade improves. USD strong → capital flight from SE Asia → currencies weaken.

**Transmission to Middle East:** USD weak → oil ↑ (denominated in USD) → Saudi, UAE, Qatar revenues ↑. USD strong → oil ↓ → petrodollar recycling slows.

## REGION 2: 🇨🇳 CHINA — THE FACTORY & CHALLENGER
### Second largest economy. Driver of commodity super-cycles. Challenger to dollar hegemony.

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Central Bank** | People's Bank of China (PBOC) |
| **Money Creation** | PBOC sets rates/RRR → state banks lend to SOEs, real estate, infrastructure |
| **Liquidity Levers** | LPR, RRR, MLF, PSL, yuan fixing |
| **Currency** | CNY/CNH — managed float, band around fixing, capital controls |
| **What It Drives** | Global commodity demand (iron ore, copper, coal, oil, soybeans), global supply chain, EM exports |
| **Key Data** | GDP, Caixin/PMI, industrial production, trade balance, social financing, property sales, yuan fixing |
| **Key Link** | China ↔ Australia (iron ore), Brazil (soy/iron), Chile (copper), Saudi (oil), NZ (dairy), LATAM (commodities), SE Asia (supply chain), Africa (resources) |

**Triggers:**
- China stimulus → commodity super-cycle → AUD, NZD, CAD, BRL, CLP, ZAR all rally
- China slowdown → commodity crash → all commodity currencies sell off
- PBOC eases → yuan weakens → EM Asia competitive devaluation
- PBOC tightens → yuan strengthens → EM Asia import deflation

**LATAM Connection:** China is Brazil's #1 trading partner. Buys soy, iron ore, crude oil, beef. China slowdown = direct hit to BRL. Same for Chile (copper), Peru (copper/zinc).

**Africa Connection:** China is Africa's largest trading partner. Buys oil from Angola, copper from DRC/Zambia, cobalt from DRC. China slowdown = African resource economies suffer.

**SE Asia Connection:** China is the hub of regional supply chains. China exports components to Vietnam/Malaysia/Thailand for final assembly. China slowdown = SE Asia exports drop.

**Middle East Connection:** China buys ~25% of Saudi oil. China-Saudi relations deepening. China brokers Iran-Saudi deal. Yuan-denominated oil contracts = slow erosion of petrodollar.

## REGION 4: 🇯🇵 JAPAN — THE CARRY TRADE ENGINE
### Last to exit negative rates. Largest foreign holder of US Treasuries.

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Central Bank** | Bank of Japan (BOJ) |
| **Money Creation** | BOJ buys JGBs, ETFs, corporate bonds. Massive QE since 2013 |
| **Liquidity Levers** | Policy rate, YCC (yield curve control), JGB purchases, ETF purchases |
| **Currency** | JPY — the ultimate funding currency for carry trades |
| **What It Drives** | USD/JPY, yen carry trade (borrow JPY → buy higher-yielding assets globally), JGB yields |
| **Key Data** | BOJ decisions, CPI, GDP, Tankan survey, trade balance, JGB 10Y yield |
| **Critical Fact** | Japan holds ~$1.1T of US Treasuries. If Japan sells → US rates rise globally. |

**The Carry Trade Mechanism:**
```
Normal:   Borrow JPY at 0.25% → Buy BRL at 14.75% = 14.5% carry
          → Also buy MXN, ZAR, INR, IDR for carry

Unwind:   Risk-off → Sell BRL, buy back JPY → BRL/JPY drops
          → More selling → cascade across all EM
```

**LATAM Connection:** JPY is the funding currency for the BRL, MXN carry trade. When BOJ hikes → carry trade unwinds → BRL, MXN sell off hardest (highest carry currencies). When BOJ stays dovish → BRL, MXN, COP carry trades remain active.

**Africa Connection:** ZAR is a high-yield carry target. BOJ policy directly affects ZAR/JPY carry.

**SE Asia Connection:** IDR is a classic carry trade target (high yield + liquid). BOJ policy = directly affects USD/IDR.

| **Primary Exports** | Soybeans (#1 exporter), iron ore (#2), crude oil, beef (#1), coffee (#1), sugar (#1), chicken (#1), ethanol |

**Triggers:**
- China stimulus → iron ore ↑ → BRL ↑ (China is Brazil's #1 trading partner)
- Oil prices ↑ → Brazil (self-sufficient oil producer) → BRL ↑

```
Borrow USD at 5% → Buy BRL at 14.75% = 9.75% carry
But BRL can move 20-30% in a year → carry can be wiped out by FX move
BRL = high yield, high risk, high vol
```

## REGION 15: 🇸🇦 SAUDI ARABIA & GULF STATES — THE PETRODOLLAR

|-----------|------------------|

| **Currencies** | All pegged to USD (SAR, AED = fixed. QAR, KWD = basket with USD anchor) |
| **What They Drive** | Oil supply (OPEC decisions), petrodollar recycling (buy US Treasuries, global assets), SWF capital (PIF $1T+, ADIA $1T+, QIA $500B+) |
| **Key Data** | OPEC production quotas, Saudi Aramco pricing, SWF deployment, Vision 2030 spending, NEOM, oil inventories |
| **Primary Exports** | Crude oil, refined products, petrochemicals, LNG (Qatar), gold (UAE hub) |

**Triggers:**
- OPEC cuts production → oil ↑ → everyone pays more at the pump → global inflation → CBs forced to hike
- OPEC increases production → oil ↓ → inflation relief → CBs can cut
- Saudi-US relationship tension → Saudi pivots to China/BRICS → petrodollar weakens → long-term USD bearish
- Saudi SWF (PIF) deploys capital → global M&A, sports, tech, infrastructure
- UAE as safe haven → Middle East instability drives capital to Dubai
- Oil ↓ below Saudi fiscal breakeven (~$85/bbl) → Saudi draws down reserves → global liquidity impact

**The Petrodollar Cycle:**
```
Saudi sells oil → Buyers pay in USD → Saudi holds USD reserves
  → Saudi buys US Treasuries → US rates lower → Global rates lower
  → USD strengthens (Treasury demand)
  
If Saudi takes yuan for oil:
  → USD demand decreases → USD long-term bearish
  → Yuan internationalization accelerates
  → Petrodollar system begins to crack
```

**Connection to Other Regions:**

- China: Saudi's #1 oil customer. Saudi sells oil to China in yuan experimentally.
- Russia: OPEC+ partner. Coordinate oil production quotas. Shared interest in oil prices.
- Europe: Major oil/gas customer before Ukraine war. Now pivoting away.
- India: Saudi's #2 oil customer. Growing strategic partnership.
- Brazil: Saudi invests in Brazil ag, infrastructure. Competitor in oil.
- Africa: Saudi invests in African agriculture, infrastructure. Competes with China.

## THE FLOW VERDICT — PER LIQUIDITY REGIME

```
FED INJECTING (TGA ↓, RRP abundant):
  → Long: AUD, NZD, CAD, BRL, MXN, ZAR, IDR
  → Short: JPY (carry), CHF (safe haven)
  → Commodities up (WTI, Gold, Copper)
  → Risk-on across all 18 regions

FED DRAINING (QT, TGA ↑, RRP exhausted):
  → Long: USD, CHF (safe haven)
  → Short: AUD, NZD, BRL, ZAR (highest beta)
  → Short: MXN, IDR, COP (EM exposure)
  → Commodities down
  → Risk-off everywhere

CHINA STIMULATING:
  → Long: AUD, NZD, CAD, BRL, CLP, ZAR, IDR
  → Commodity super-cycle (copper, iron ore, WTI, coal, nickel)
  → Brazil iron ore + soy → BRL
  → Chile copper → CLP
  → Indonesia coal/nickel → IDR

CHINA SLOWING:
  → Short: AUD, NZD, BRL, CLP, ZAR, IDR
  → Commodity crash
  → EM currencies under pressure

BOJ HIKING / YCC EXIT:
  → Short: All carry pairs (long JPY across board)
  → Biggest impact: BRL/JPY, ZAR/JPY, MXN/JPY, AUD/JPY
  → Global risk-off as carry trades unwind

RISK-OFF (Geopolitical, Credit Event):
  → Long: USD, CHF, Gold
  → Short: EUR if Europe exposed
  → Short: AUD, NZD, ZAR, BRL, MXN, IDR (EM + commodity)
  → EM currencies sell off in order: ZAR > BRL > MXN > IDR > INR

COMMODITY SUPER-CYCLE:
  → Long: AUD, NZD, CAD, BRL, CLP, ZAR, IDR, MYR, COP, RUB
  → Long: WTI, Copper, Gold, Coal, Nickel, Palm Oil
  → Short: JPY (energy importer), INR (oil importer), EUR (manufacturing)
```
