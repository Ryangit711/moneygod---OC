## THE THREE REGIONS

### 🇺🇸 REGION 1: US — THE ENGINE (Primary Focus)
*Money is printed here. Everything else receives the overflow.*

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Central Bank** | Federal Reserve (Fed) — most powerful financial institution on Earth |
| **Money Creation** | Fed creates reserves → banks create broad money via lending |
| **Key Liquidity Levers** | Fed Funds Rate, QE/QT, TGA balance, RRP facility, Discount Window |
| **Dominant Currency** | USD — global reserve, 88% of FX turnover, all commodities priced in it |
| **What to Trade** | USD pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY), US equities (SPX, NDX), US Treasuries (ZN, ZB) |
| **Key Data** | NFP, CPI, PCE, FOMC minutes, TGA, Reverse Repo, Fed balance sheet |
| **Edge** | Dollar liquidity is the tide that lifts (or sinks) all boats |

**Core Identity:** USD = the world's risk barometer.
- When USD weakens → risk-on → commodities up, equities up, EM up
- When USD strengthens → risk-off → everything down except US treasuries
- When USD liquidity is abundant (Fed injecting) → everything rallies
- When USD liquidity drains (QT, TGA rising) → everything suffers

### 🇨🇦 REGION 2: CANADA — THE HOME BASE
*Commodity satellite. Dollar liquidity flows through oil & gas.*

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Central Bank** | Bank of Canada (BoC) |
| **Money Creation** | BoC creates reserves → Canadian banks create CAD via lending |
| **Key Liquidity Levers** | Overnight rate, BoC balance sheet, housing market, commodity prices |
| **Dominant Pairs** | USD/CAD (most traded), EUR/CAD, GBP/CAD, CAD/JPY |
| **What to Trade** | USD/CAD, WTI crude (CL), nat gas (NG), gold (GC), TSX60 |
| **Key Data** | BoC rate decisions, GDP, employment, housing starts, trade balance, WTI inventory |
| **Canadian Edge** | CAD = petrocurrency. WTI drives CAD more than any rate decision. |
| **Tax Shields** | TFSA, RRSP, FHSA — compound tax-free |

**Core Identity:** CAD = a commodity proxy with a central bank that follows the Fed.
- BoC rarely diverges from Fed (divergence crushes CAD)
- When WTI ↑ → CAD ↑ → USD/CAD ↓
- Canada's housing market is the biggest domestic risk (debt-to-income 187%)
- Canadian dollar is a "risk-on" currency — rallies when global growth is strong

### 🇪🇺 REGION 3: EUROPE — THE SECONDARY FLOW
*Old world. Where dollar liquidity goes to hide or chase yield.*

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Central Bank** | European Central Bank (ECB) |
| **Money Creation** | ECB creates euros via TLTROs, APP, PEPP — banks lend, but credit growth is slower |
| **Key Liquidity Levers** | ECB deposit rate, APP/ PEPP, TLTRO repayments, bund yields, periphery spreads |
| **Dominant Pairs** | EUR/USD (most traded pair in the world), GBP/USD, EUR/CHF, USD/CHF |
| **What to Trade** | EUR/USD, GBP/USD, Gold (GC), Bunds (FGBL), Euro Stoxx 50 |
| **Key Data** | ECB rate decisions, Eurozone PMI, German GDP, CPI, TLTRO activity |
| **European Edge** | EUR/USD is the primary transmission of dollar liquidity into Europe |

**Core Identity:** EUR = the anti-USD.
- EUR/USD is the single most traded currency pair (28% of all FX volume)
- When dollar liquidity is flowing into Europe → EUR/USD ↑
- When Europe has its own crisis (debt, energy, migration) → EUR/USD ↓
- CHF and GBP are satellites: CHF = safe haven, GBP = risk proxy with Brexit premium

## THE CANADIAN TRADER'S TRI-REGION STRATEGY

### What to Watch First (Priority Order)
1. **Fed liquidity** — TGA, RRP, Fed balance sheet → this drives everything
2. **WTI inventory** — EIA Wednesday → drives CAD immediately
3. **EUR/USD** — the global risk barometer pair → tells you where dollar flow is going
4. **BoC vs Fed divergence** — when they diverge, CAD moves violently
5. **ECB vs Fed divergence** — when they diverge, EUR/USD trends for months

### Core Positions
| Environment | Trade | Rationale |
|-------------|-------|-----------|
| Fed injecting, WTI rising | Short USD/CAD | Dollar flows + oil up = CAD up |
| QT + risk-off + WTI falling | Long USD/CAD | Dollar liquidity draining = USD up |
| Fed injecting, Europe stable | Long EUR/USD | Dollar flows into Europe |
| QT + Europe crisis | Short EUR/USD | Dollar safe haven, EUR sells off |
| Risk-on + commodity boom | Long CAD/JPY | Carry + commodity proxy |
| Risk-off + uncertainty | Short EUR/CHF | CHF safe haven, EUR risk proxy |
