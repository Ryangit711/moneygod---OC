# CURRICULUM — From Zero to Funded Trader

## Your Study Path

This is the front door. Everything in this repo exists to serve this curriculum. Follow it in order. Do not skip phases. Do not rush to trading.

**Total time:** ~3 months to funded eval readiness. ~6 months to consistent.
**Daily commitment:** 1 hr (Phase 1-4) → 2-3 hrs (Phase 5-6).
**Ultimate goal:** Read global flow → position on any asset, from anywhere, via phone, with positive expectancy.

| 15 | `core/global-flow-map.md` (introduction + US/Canada sections) | Find Canada in the transmission table. How does dollar flow affect CAD? |

**Real data practice (15 min):**
Go to **fred.stlouisfed.org** and look up:
- `WRESBAL` (reserve balances)
- `RRPONTSYD` (ON RRP facility)
- `WTREGEN` (TGA balance)
- Compute: Net Liq = ΔBS − ΔTGA − ΔRRP
- Write the verdict: NET LIQ IN / NET LIQ OUT / NEUTRAL

## PHASE 5 — Demo Trading (Weeks 7-12)

**Goal:** 60 demo trades with positive expectancy. Screen time + scar tissue.

**Time:** 2-3 hrs per day. This is your part-time job now.

### Pre-Demo Prep

| Day | Read | Do |
|-----|------|-----|
| 38 | `quickstart/pip-calculator.md` | Calculate pip values for all major pairs at 0.01, 0.10, and 1.00 lot sizes. |
| 39 | `trading/complete-strategy-orb-eurusd.md` (full) | Set up your chart template as described: session lines, VWAP, ORB zones. |
| 40 | `quickstart/02-platform-setup.md` | Place your **first demo trade** — EUR/USD, 0.01 lot, buy or sell. Set SL and TP. |
| 41 | `quickstart/03-demo-path.md` (Week 1) | Do not make a second trade yet. Wait. Watch. Journal what happened. |

### Demo Routine (Days 42-75)

**Every trading day (follow `systems/3hr-daily-schedule.md`):**
```
:00-:15 — Live data workflow (systems/live-data-workflow.md)
:16-:20 — Gangotri Protocol (systems/gangotri-protocol.md) — edge matrix + 3-question gate
:21-:25 — Commandments read + plumbing-to-trade-bridge decision trees
:26-:60 — Execute per instrument playbook (MES/MNQ or FX per bridge verdict)
:60-:70 — Post-session review + journal
:70-:90 — Study time (COT, hierarchy-master review, or syllabus work)
```

**Week-by-week:**

| Week | Focus | Max Trades | Target |
|------|-------|------------|--------|
| Week 7 | Just watch + paper trade in journal | 0 demo trades | Understand session behavior |
| Week 8 | 1-2 demo trades per day, EUR/USD only | 10 total | No blowing demo in first 10 trades |
| Week 9 | Add USD/CAD (ORB strategy) | 10-15 total | Positive expectancy over last 20 |
| Week 10 | Add /ES futures (1 contract) | 10-15 total | All 3 instruments feel natural |
| Week 11 | Full routine, all setups | 15-20 total | 60-trade sample by end |
| Week 12 | Review + prepare for prop eval | Taper to 5-10 | Identify strengths |

### Books to Read During Demo Phase

| Read When | Book | Why |
|-----------|------|-----|
| Week 7-8 | *Reminiscences of a Stock Operator* — Edwin Lefèvre | Every trader's mistake is in this 1923 book. You will make them too. |
| Week 9-10 | *Market Wizards* — Jack Schwager | See how the greats think. You'll find your style by seeing theirs. |
| Week 10-11 | *Trade Your Way to Financial Freedom* — Van Tharp | Position sizing + system design. Read before prop eval. |
| Week 11-12 | *Fooled by Randomness* — Taleb | You need this before you attribute skill to luck. |

### Demo Phase Milestones

| Check | How to Know You're Ready |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **30 consecutive days** | You have not skipped a single day of pre-session + journal |
| **60+ trades** | Not 10. Not 20. 60. Sample size matters. |
| **Positive expectancy** | Your total P&L is positive, AND your average win > average loss |
| **Max drawdown < 10%** | You never lost more than 10% of your demo account peak |
| **No revenge trading** | You have at least one journal entry where you walked away after a loss |
| **Strategy fidelity** | You followed your rules on 90%+ of trades |

If all 6 are true → proceed to Phase 6. If any is false → stay in Phase 5 until all 6 pass.

## Daily Ritual (Once You're In Phases 5-6)

This is your default day. Do this until it becomes unconscious.

```
MORNING (15 min)
  ☐ Daily pre-session (systems/daily-pre-session.md)
  ☐ Check plumbing indicators (plumbing-esoterica insider check)
  ☐ Mark session times + levels on chart
  ☐ Set alarms for session opens / news

SESSION (1-3 hrs)
  ☐ Execute only if your setup triggers
  ☐ Size by liquidity regime
  ☐ If no setup → watch + journal
  ☐ One trade at a time. No revenge. No doubling.

POST-SESSION (10 min)
  ☐ Journal every trade
  ☐ Screenshot chart with entries/exits
  ☐ Write: what worked, what didn't, what to change

WEEKLY (Monday, 15 min)
  ☐ Full plumbing check (plumbing-esoterica insider check)
  ☐ Liquidity verdict for the week
  ☐ Review last week's journal
  ☐ Check: did I follow the commandments?

MONTHLY (1st of month, 30 min)
  ☐ P&L review
  ☐ Strategy adjustment? Market regime change?
  ☐ Read one of: 10-investing, 11-escape, 12-who-knows
  ☐ Update your level on the 10-level mastery path
```

## The Only Rule That Matters

> **If you open a trade without doing the pre-session, you are gambling.**
>
> **If you open a trade without a journal entry planned, you are gambling.**
>
> **If you open a trade just because you're bored, you are gambling.**

The pre-session, the journal, the plumbing check — these are NOT optional extras. They are the **minimum viable discipline** for reading the global flow. Skip them and you will not read the flow. You will be reading your own emotions.
