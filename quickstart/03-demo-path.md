# 30-DAY DEMO PATH — ZERO TO DEMO READY
## "You don't need to be ready to start. You need to start to be ready."

---

## THE MINDSET

For the next 30 days, you are not trying to make money. You are trying to learn how the screen moves.

**Goal Day 30:** You can open MT5, read a USD/CAD chart, identify a setup, place a trade with proper stop loss and take profit, and journal what happened. No profit target. No loss that matters. Just learning.

**The only rule that matters for 30 days:** Never risk more than 1% of demo account on any single trade. Not for practice. Not for fun. Train the habit now.

---

## WEEK 1: EXPLORATION (Days 1-7)
### "What does the screen even show?"

| Day | Activity | Time | Done? |
|-----|----------|------|-------|
| **1** | Download TradingView + MT5. Open both. Spend 30 min just looking. | 30 min | ☐ |
| **2** | Watch USD/CAD for 1 hour during NY session (7am-10am PT). Don't trade. Just watch. Note: what time did it move? | 1 hr | ☐ |
| **3** | Mark 3 levels on USD/CAD chart: support, resistance, current price. Watch price touch them. | 30 min | ☐ |
| **4** | Place ONE demo trade on USD/CAD. 0.01 micro lot. Set SL + TP. Let it play out. | 15 min | ☐ |
| **5** | Watch EUR/USD for 1 hour (London open, midnight-3am PT or NY open 5am). Note: how is it different from USD/CAD? | 1 hr | ☐ |
| **6** | Place ONE more demo trade. Any pair. Same rules: 0.01 lot, SL + TP. | 15 min | ☐ |
| **7** | Review your 2 trades. What did you do well? What was confusing? | 15 min | ☐ |

**Week 1 Lesson:** You don't need to know everything. You just need to know what you don't know. By day 7, you should know: what time the screen moves, what a pip looks like, how to place an order.

---

## WEEK 2: RHYTHM (Days 8-14)
### "Finding your session, your pair, your pace"

| Day | Activity | Time | Done? |
|-----|----------|------|-------|
| **8** | Pre-session checklist (read systems/daily-pre-session.md → fill it out for today) | 5 min | ☐ |
| **9** | Trade 1 session only (NY session 5am-10am PT) — sit and watch, mark entries you WOULD have taken | 2 hr | ☐ |
| **10** | Trade 1 demo trade with a plan (bias + entry + stop + target written BEFORE you click) | 15 min | ☐ |
| **11** | Post-session review: what did the flow tell you? Read today's global check. | 10 min | ☐ |
| **12** | Demo trade. Focus on execution — did you enter where you planned? | 15 min | ☐ |
| **13** | Watch WTI crude during EIA (Wednesday 10:30 AM ET = 7:30am PT). Watch how USD/CAD moves with it. | 1 hr | ☐ |
| **14** | Week 2 review: count your trades. Did you follow your plan? | 15 min | ☐ |

**Week 2 Lesson:** Consistency beats intensity. One session, one pair, one setup. Repetition is how you build instinct.

---

## WEEK 3: STRUCTURE (Days 15-21)
### "Levels, structure, reading the battlefield"

| Day | Activity | Time | Done? |
|-----|----------|------|-------|
| **15** | Mark HTF structure on USD/CAD D1: trend direction, key support, key resistance | 10 min | ☐ |
| **16** | Mark same on 4H. Combine D1 bias + 4H levels → enter | 15 min | ☐ |
| **17** | Demo trade based on structure (not random — you have a reason) | 15 min | ☐ |
| **18** | Read [`first-setup-orb.md`] — understand ORB (Opening Range Breakout) | 20 min | ☐ |
| **19** | Apply ORB on today's session — find the 15-min range, watch for breakout | 1 hr | ☐ |
| **20** | Demo trade using ORB setup only | 15 min | ☐ |
| **21** | Week 3 review: how many of your trades were ORB-based? Did structure hold? | 15 min | ☐ |

**Week 3 Lesson:** You don't need 20 strategies. You need 1 strategy you understand well enough to execute without thinking.

---

## WEEK 4: DISCIPLINE (Days 22-30)
### "Systems, habits, preparation"

| Day | Activity | Time | Done? |
|-----|----------|------|-------|
| **22** | Full pre-session checklist (5 min) + 1 demo trade | 20 min | ☐ |
| **23** | Read [`commandments.md`] — your 10 trading rules. Read them before every session. | 5 min | ☐ |
| **24** | Demo trade with full journal entry (see [`trade-journal-template.md`]) | 20 min | ☐ |
| **25** | No trading day. Read the global flow map. What's happening in China? US? EU? | 30 min | ☐ |
| **26** | Demo trade. Focus on risk: 0.01 lot, 1% max loss. Do you respect it? | 15 min | ☐ |
| **27** | Review all your trades from weeks 1-4. Count: wins, losses, what worked. | 30 min | ☐ |
| **28** | Demo trade. This time: let a winner run (trailing stop). | 15 min | ☐ |
| **29** | Full practice run: pre-session → global check → plan trade → execute → journal | 30 min | ☐ |
| **30** | **GRADUATION:** You have a system. You can read charts. You can place a trade. You know your setup. | — | ☐ |

**Week 4 Lesson:** Discipline > Prediction. The trader who follows their rules every day will outperform the genius who doesn't.

## THE 30-DAY TRADE LOG

Create a file called `data/trade-log.md` or just track in a notebook:

```
| # | Date | Pair | Direction | Entry | Exit | Result | R | Setup | Lesson |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | D4 | USD/CAD | Long | 1.3600 | 1.3580 | -20 pips | -1R | Random | Set SL next time |
| 2 | D6 | EUR/USD | Short | 1.0800 | 1.0780 | +20 pips | +1R | Random | TP auto-closed good |
| ... |
```

**By day 30, aim for 15-20 logged trades.** Not 100. Quality > quantity. Each trade should teach you something.

## THE RULE THAT SUPERSEDES ALL

**If you miss a day, do not skip to the next day.**
**Do today's activity. The next day is tomorrow.**

Consistency is the only thing that works. One missed day is fine. Five missed days in a row means you need to restart Week 1.
