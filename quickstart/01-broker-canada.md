## HOW TO OPEN EACH (Step by Step)

### Interactive Brokers (IBKR) — Canada
1. Go to interactivebrokers.ca
2. Click "Open Account" → Individual
3. Account type: **Individual Margin** (or Cash if under $5000)
4. Base currency: **CAD**
5. Funded account tier: choose based on deposit
6. KYC: SIN number, photo ID, proof of address
7. Funding: Bill Pay, wire, or CAD EFT from your Canadian bank
8. Wait 2-5 business days for approval

### OANDA (Beginner-friendly)
1. Go to oanda.com/ca-en
2. Open "CAD Account"
3. Email, password, personal info, SIN
4. Deposit via Interac e-Transfer or wire
5. Start trading same day

### Goat Funded Trader (Prop Firm)
1. Go to goatfundedtrader.com
2. Choose Challenge → e.g. $15k account for ~$120 CAD
3. Create account, pay via crypto/credit card
4. Download MT5 → login with challenge credentials
5. Pass the 10% target → get funded account

## ACCOUNT TYPES FOR CANADIANS

| Type | Use For | Tax Implication |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **TFSA** | Long-term investing (stocks/ETFs) | Tax-free growth. **CANNOT trade frequently** — CRA considers it business income. |
| **RRSP** | Long-term retirement | Tax-deferred. Same trading restriction issue. |
| **FHSA** | First home savings | Tax-free. Same restriction. |
| **Margin Account (IBKR/OANDA)** | Day trading forex/futures | Capital gains taxed at 50%. Day trading = business income (100% taxable). |
| **Prop Firm Account** | Funded trading | Profit split is income. Taxable at marginal rate. |

**Critical:** Do NOT day trade inside TFSA/RRSP/FHSA. CRA will reassess as business income and tax you. Use margin account or prop firm for active trading. Use TFSA for long-term buy-and-hold only.
