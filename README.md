## The Core Premise

[Money](03-money-as-[debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md)-modern-system.md) and [debt](01-origins-of-money-and-debt.md) are not natural — they are engineered systems of obligation. The word "economy" originally meant **[household management](02-the-real-meaning-of-economy.md)** ([oikonomia](02-the-real-meaning-of-economy.md)), not GDP growth. Most people never learn this. The people who do use it to their advantage. This wiki is about joining them.
